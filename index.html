Lifestyle & Risk Comparison

Keeping Personal Car

  • Pocket Savings: Keep a larger cash buffer for rent/savings.
  • No BIK Tax: Avoid monthly benefit-in-kind payroll deductions.
  • Freedom: Your car is yours; no contract restrictions or mileage limits from a leasing company.
  • Asset Ownership: You own the residual value of the car (if you sell it later).

Taking Company Car

  • Zero Risk: Breakdown, engine failure, gearbox replacement? All €0 cost to you.
  • Stress-Free: No garage bills, tire shopping, or dealing with road tax paperwork.
  • Brand New Vehicle: Drive a modern, safer, and cleaner vehicle with modern infotainment/safety systems.
  • Predictable Budget: Exactly the same salary impact every single month, no surprises.
